Achievements

RVCE ranked 2nd among Technical Institutions selected for the World Bank supported TEQIP Phase II Sub Comp: 1.2, facilitated by MHRD GOI. World Bank supported TEQIP Phase II – Centre of Excellence in Macroelectronics for 3 years (2015-2018). RVCE ranked as the top institute (No:1) in the country among the self financed engineering for Return on Investment(RoI) / Value for Money for students by Outlook in June 2013. RVCE is also No 1 in the area of sport under VTU in Karnataka among engineering colleges. Ministry of Human Resources Development (GoI) chose RVCE for showcasing India’s new Accreditation process during the review by International Observers. Strong R&D focus: Completed over 100 projects, The institute has about 45 Ongoing projects from various funding agencies including DST, DBT, DRDO and its affiliated agencies, UGC, VTU, AICTE, CII, and VGST Karnataka ( a transformation from about Rs.7 Crores in 47 years to close to Rs 45 Crores of approvals in 3 years).

NSS-contributions

1.Utsarga Marathon (5th June) This is one of the flagship event of our club , this year we had conducted a marathon for the cause of education and eradication of child labour , in this event we had collected funds from staffs and students to participate in the marathon and donated the same to education of under privileged children.

2.Har Gharana Tiranga (13,14,1th august) To support the Government to achieve its goal and to make sure that RVCE be a part of this campaign, NSS RVCE has taken initiative to distribute National Flags to the students, faculty and staff at Rs.22

3.Eco-friendly Ganesha Making-Bengaluru Ganesh Utsav (28th August) A total of 3560 participants participated in the event and successfully made their clay Ganesha. After the event it was declared that a new Guinness World Record had been set for the largest number of participants making clay Ganesha in a limited time. It was declared as a new world record by Guinness World Record adjudicator Rishi Nath. Later participation certificates were given to the participants and certificates are to be given to volunteers as well. 3 activity points are to be provided to both participants and volunteers from RVCE.
