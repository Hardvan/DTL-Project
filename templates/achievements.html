Achievements

RVCE ranked 2nd among Technical Institutions selected for the World Bank supported TEQIP Phase II Sub Comp: 1.2, facilitated by MHRD GOI. World Bank supported TEQIP Phase II – Centre of Excellence in Macroelectronics for 3 years (2015-2018). RVCE ranked as the top institute (No:1) in the country among the self financed engineering for Return on Investment(RoI) / Value for Money for students by Outlook in June 2013. RVCE is also No 1 in the area of sport under VTU in Karnataka among engineering colleges. Ministry of Human Resources Development (GoI) chose RVCE for showcasing India’s new Accreditation process during the review by International Observers. Strong R&D focus: Completed over 100 projects, The institute has about 45 Ongoing projects from various funding agencies including DST, DBT, DRDO and its affiliated agencies, UGC, VTU, AICTE, CII, and VGST Karnataka ( a transformation from about Rs.7 Crores in 47 years to close to Rs 45 Crores of approvals in 3 years).
